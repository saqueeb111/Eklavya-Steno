How to Use Stenogram

Master the ultimate platform designed to mirror official SSC, AIIMS, and DSSSB exam environments.

1 The Dictation Library

Navigate to the Dictations tab. Use the search bar or category tabs to find official Kailash Chandra, GD Bisht, or Legal matters.

Speed Control on the Fly:

Click any dictation card to enter the workspace. Use the dropdown inside the audio player to dynamically slow down or speed up the dictation from 60 WPM to 120 WPM without distorting the voice.

2 The Steno Analyzer (Core)

The heart of Stenogram. While it perfectly links with our online dictations, The Custom Matter Advantage allows you to use it for offline practice too!

Took a dictation at your coaching institute? Simply paste the original answer key in the left box, paste your typed text in the right box, and hit analyze for an instant SSC-grade evaluation.

The Partial Analyser:

Only managed to transcribe half the dictation? Check the Partial Analyser box. The system will intelligently find where you stopped and only evaluate the words you typed, saving you from massive omission penalties.

3 Pro Typing Test

Preparing for LDC/Typing tests for SSC, AIIMS, DSSSB, or Supreme Court? Our typing tab provides an exact exam-like interface.

Feedback Styles:

  • Normal Mode: Strict exam style. No colors, no hints. You only see your WPM and Accuracy at the end.
  • Live Colors Mode: Perfect for practice. The text turns Green when correct and Red when you make a typo, allowing you to fix finger memory mistakes instantly.
